{%gathering_year%} Spring Gathering Details

MSF gatherings are free and open to all. If you have never attended one of our spring or fall gatherings, they are not like most programmed Christian conferences, where speakers and activities are planned out in advance. Most of our sessions are just times of gathering together, waiting upon the Lord in silence, and then sharing, teaching, exhorting, praying, etc. as we feel specifically led by the Spirit of God. Some meetings may pass by entirely in silence as each individual seeks to receive from the life-giving presence of the Lord in his or her own heart. The afternoon sessions are times dedicated to teaching, followed by questions or discussion with those who are present or joining us online.
